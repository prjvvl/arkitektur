Project Description

This contemporary residence designed for Mr. Siddharth Nimbalkar reflects modern architectural principles while being sensitive to the local climate and context. The 3-bedroom villa strikes a perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality, creating comfortable living spaces that enhance the quality of life for the inhabitants.

The design philosophy was centered around three key principles:

  • Maximizing natural light and ventilation
  • Creating fluid spatial connections between various zones of the house
  • Establishing a strong indoor-outdoor relationship

The façade features a combination of clean lines, textured surfaces, and thoughtful material selection. Large windows allow ample daylight to penetrate deep into the living spaces while providing framed views of the surrounding landscape. The double-height living area creates a sense of openness and serves as the focal point of the home.

The interior spaces are designed with a minimalist approach, emphasizing quality over quantity. Premium materials and finishes have been carefully selected to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. The color palette is predominantly neutral, allowing the architectural elements and carefully curated furniture pieces to stand out.
